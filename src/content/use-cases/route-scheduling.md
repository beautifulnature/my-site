## What is Route Scheduling Optimization?

Route scheduling optimization finds the best possible routes for a fleet of vehicles serving a set of locations. Rather than relying on static plans or human intuition, Timefold continuously evaluates millions of possible route combinations and selects the one that minimizes your chosen objective — whether that is total distance, cost, emissions, or driver overtime.

The solver respects hard constraints such as vehicle capacities, time windows, and driver regulations, while optimizing across soft goals like customer priority and fuel efficiency.

## Key Capabilities

- **Real-time re-optimization** — Recompute routes instantly when orders are added, cancelled, or traffic changes
- **Multi-vehicle, multi-depot** — Coordinate large fleets across many depots or warehouses
- **Time-window enforcement** — Guarantee on-time arrivals within agreed delivery windows
- **Capacity constraints** — Respect weight, volume, and compartment limits per vehicle
- **Driver regulations** — Enforce mandatory breaks and maximum working-hour rules automatically

## How It Works

1. Submit your locations, vehicles, and constraints via a single API call
2. Timefold's planning engine runs constraint-based search in the cloud
3. An optimized schedule is returned in seconds (or milliseconds for incremental updates)
4. Integrate the result directly into your dispatch or TMS system
