## What is Shift Scheduling Optimization?

Shift scheduling optimization automatically builds employee rosters that simultaneously satisfy business demand, individual preferences, and labor regulations. Timefold replaces time-consuming manual spreadsheet work with a solver that evaluates every possible assignment and returns the fairest, most cost-effective schedule instantly.

The solver treats compliance constraints (working-time directives, mandatory rest periods) as hard rules that are never violated, while it optimizes soft goals like employee satisfaction, fairness, and overtime minimization.

## Key Capabilities

- **Demand-driven scheduling** — Match staffing levels precisely to forecast workloads across every shift
- **Skills and certifications** — Assign only qualified employees to roles that require specific credentials
- **Preference satisfaction** — Honor time-off requests and shift preferences wherever possible
- **Labor law compliance** — Automatically enforce maximum hours, minimum rest, and consecutive-day limits
- **Fairness scoring** — Distribute unsocial hours and weekend duties equitably across the team
- **Real-time re-planning** — Handle last-minute absences with instant re-optimization

## How It Works

1. Provide your employee roster, shift requirements, and constraint rules via the API
2. The solver generates a compliant, optimized schedule in seconds
3. Review and publish the schedule in your WFM or HR platform
4. Re-run at any time to incorporate changes or absence notifications
