## What is Task Scheduling Optimization?

Task scheduling optimization assigns a dynamic workload of tasks to the most suitable available resources — people, machines, or a combination — while respecting every constraint in your business. Timefold handles the combinatorial complexity so your dispatchers and planners can focus on exceptions rather than building schedules by hand.

Tasks are sequenced to minimize idle time, travel, and deadline violations. When new high-priority work arrives, the schedule is re-optimized in real time without manual intervention.

## Key Capabilities

- **Skill-based assignment** — Match each task to a resource that has the right skills, tools, or certifications
- **Priority and deadline management** — Guarantee that critical tasks are completed on time even as new work arrives
- **Location-aware sequencing** — Minimize travel between tasks for field teams
- **Dependency handling** — Respect task chains where one job must finish before another can start
- **Resource availability** — Account for working hours, breaks, leave, and concurrent commitments
- **Incremental re-optimization** — Slot new urgent tasks into the live schedule without disrupting existing assignments

## How It Works

1. Define your task list, resources, skills, and business rules via the API
2. Timefold returns an optimal task-to-resource assignment within seconds
3. Push the schedule to your field-service, MES, or ticketing system
4. Add or escalate tasks at any time — the solver re-plans automatically
