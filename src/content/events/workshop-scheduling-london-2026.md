# Hands-on Workshop – Scheduling in Practice

A full-day workshop on building and tuning scheduling models.

## Topics

- From spreadsheet schedules to constraint models  
- Trade-offs between hard and soft constraints  
- Evaluating schedules with KPIs

## Who it’s for

Engineering leaders, operations managers, and technical teams working on complex schedules.
